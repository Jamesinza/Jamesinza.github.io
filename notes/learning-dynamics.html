Learning Dynamics

What the loss curve does not tell you.

Focus

This note is about how models learn, not just what they converge to.

Observed Behaviors

  • early representation locking
  • sensitivity to initialization far beyond expectation
  • learning rate schedules acting as implicit regularizers
  • sudden phase transitions in internal structure

Key Observation

Two models with identical final accuracy can arrive there through radically different trajectories.

Those trajectories determine robustness.

Implication

Monitoring loss alone is insufficient. Understanding learning dynamics is critical for diagnosing instability and hidden brittleness.
